## 🧩 **Diagnosis Summary**

### 1. **Duplicate UI**

You currently have **two separate components** mounting:

* `IncomingCallModal` or similar overlay (blurred background, call buttons)
* `WebRTCCall` UI (the full call interface with mute/video/hangup)

These are both being rendered at once because:

* The callee automatically mounts `WebRTCCall` *and* still shows the modal instead of unmounting it when they accept.
* Or both `incoming-call` and `call-accepted` events are being handled simultaneously, triggering two renders.

🛠 **Fix Strategy**
We’ll move to a **single unified call state controller** (see below).

---

### 2. **Audio Not Heard (Both Sides)**

When the callee answers, the offer/answer exchange completes, but:

* One or both sides never call `pc.addTrack(localStreamTrack, stream)` properly, **or**
* `setRemoteDescription()` was called before tracks were added, **or**
* Local media (`getUserMedia`) wasn’t re-initialized on the receiver (common on answer side).

🧠 Likely:
The **callee never adds local tracks** to the `RTCPeerConnection` before sending the answer.
So both peers connect, but no audio/video is transmitted.
